Overview

This repository contains documentation and technical specifications for 9 AI governance patents filed between September and November 2025. These patents form a comprehensive framework for trustworthy, explainable, and compliant AI systems.

Total Patents Filed: 9 (8 provisional + 1 mathematical foundations patent)
Filing Period: September 16 - November 6, 2025
Focus Areas: AI Governance, Hybrid Human-AI Systems, Ethical AI, Mathematical Foundations
